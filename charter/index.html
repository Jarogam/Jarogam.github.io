Say goodbye to being in a foreign country and being unable to charge your laptop or phone … or to damaging a device by accidently plugging it into an incompatible outlet.

How Does It Work?

The ModernistLook International Travel Adapter has an 8-hole socket for a variety of devices and sliding plug pins
that allow you to use it with USA, UK, AUS and EURO outlets. This adapter also has 4 USB/Type C 3A smart
charging ports, 3 PCBs and it comes with two fuses (one is a backup).

To use the adapter:

  • For USA Plugs Pull out the USA compatible pins and click
    the safety lock

  • For AUS Plugs Pull out the USA pins and rotate them 60 degrees
    to the AUS plug setting and click the safety lock

  • For EURO Plugs Pull out the EURO pins and click the safety lock

  • For UK Plugs Pull out the UK pins and click the safety switch

It’s that easy to use! Any confusion over what plugs are OK to use in different countries is eliminated with this adapter. Now it couldn’t be any simpler to safely charge your devices.
